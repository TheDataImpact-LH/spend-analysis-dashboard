📋 Executive Summary · FY 2024
Spend Performance
Total procurement spend reached $48.7M against a $49.8M budget — 2.2% favorable variance driven by supplier consolidation and renegotiated Facility Services contracts.
Savings Delivered
SQL-driven spend analysis identified $4.9M in savings opportunities (10% of total). $2.8M already captured via supplier reduction and FBDI automation; $2.1M in pipeline.
Operational Efficiency
PO cycle time reduced 20% to 3.2 days through Blanket PO standardization and Oracle workflow automation. Supplier base reduced by 18 through strategic consolidation.
